# Expectations

ITAC students represent the university and the Center in client facilities, in meetings, and in every report that goes out the door. This section sets the standards for how you are expected to conduct yourself and handle the information you are trusted with.

The pages cover professional and academic conduct in the [Code of Conduct](../code-of-conduct.md), reliability and presence under [Attendance](../attendance.md) and the [Dress Code](../dress-code.md), and how and where work can be done in the [Remote Work Policy](../remote-work.md). Two pages carry consequences serious enough to end your time in the program: [Client Confidentiality](../client-confidentiality.md) governs the client information you see, and [Reporting Integrity](../reporting-integrity.md) governs the honesty of the data and analysis you produce.
