# Getting Paid

ITAC students are paid employees of the University of Connecticut. This section covers the two administrative tasks that keep you paid correctly and on time.

[Entering Hours](../entering-hours.md) explains how to log the time you work, and [Mileage Reimbursement](../mileage-reimbursement.md) walks through claiming travel to and from onsite assessments through UConn's Concur system. Submit both promptly — late entries hold up your own pay and create extra work for the staff who process them.
