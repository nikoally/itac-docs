# Onsite Assessments

Onsite assessments are the core of ITAC's work and the part of the program with the most physical risk. Before you join a visit, you must complete the safety trainings listed under [Required Trainings](../required-trainings.md) and meet the [Dress Code](../dress-code.md) and PPE requirements for the floor.

This section covers what is expected of you on a client site. [Safety Expectations](../safety-expectations.md) is the page to read first: it explains how to follow host facility rules, when to stay out of restricted areas, and your right to step away from any task you believe is unsafe.
