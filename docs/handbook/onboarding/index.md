# Onboarding

Welcome to ITAC. This section covers everything you need to do before you start contributing to assessments and reports: the first steps to take, the trainings that gate different parts of the work, and the equipment and apparel issued to active team members.

Work through it in order. Begin with [Getting Started](../getting-started.md) for your first-week checklist, then complete the [Required Trainings](../required-trainings.md) — several of which must be on file before you can touch client reports, join a site visit, or use AI tools. [Branded Apparel](../apparel.md) and [iPad Issuance](../ipad.md) cover the gear provided to active members and what is expected of you in return.
