# Advancement

Advancement at ITAC is earned through demonstrated competence, not time served. This section explains how to move up and what formal recognition is available when you do.

[Promotions](../promotion.md) describes the self-initiated process for advancing from Class II to Class III to Class IV, including the competency matrix your application is measured against. The [DOE Certificate](../certificate.md) of Participation is the national credential issued to students who meet the Department of Energy's minimum criteria; finishing Class III lines up with eligibility.
