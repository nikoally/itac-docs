# Best Practices

The Best Practices section is found under **Report Sections** → **Best-Practices.tex** in the LaTeX sidebar. It presents a curated list of the energy efficiency and manufacturing practices already in place at the facility, giving the client credit for what they are doing well before the report moves into recommendations.

## What to Do

The LaTeX file contains the full list of possible best practices as pre-written bullet points. **Do not write new entries**, simply delete the lines that do not apply to this facility. What remains should reflect only what was actually observed or confirmed.


## How to Decide What to Keep

Base your selections on information gathered from three sources, in order of reliability:

1. **On-site assessment** — what you directly observed during the facility walkthrough

2. **Pre-assessment meetings** — details shared by the facility contact beforehand

3. **Any supplemental information** obtained from the client (data sheets, utility bills, facility documentation, etc.)


If you are unsure whether a practice applies (for example, you recall seeing something on the walkthrough but aren't certain) consult the **Student Lead** or other students who attended the assessment before including or removing that item. It is better to ask than to misrepresent what the facility is doing.

Only include practices that were **confirmed** for this facility. Do not leave in items simply because they seem plausible or common for the industry. Every bullet that remains is an implicit claim that this practice is in place.
